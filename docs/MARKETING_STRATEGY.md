## Target Audiences

### Primary Targets

**1. Financial Services**
- Banks, credit unions
- Insurance companies
- Investment firms
- Accounting firms
- Regulatory compliance officers

**Pain Points:**
- Regulatory compliance (SOX, Basel III)
- Audit trail requirements
- Fraud prevention
- Document tampering concerns

**Value Proposition:**
- Cryptographic integrity (built-in)
- Audit trails (automatic)
- Regulatory compliance (tamper-evidence)

**2. Legal & Contracts**
- Law firms
- Corporate legal departments
- Contract management companies
- Notaries
- Court systems

**Pain Points:**
- Legal enforceability
- Document authenticity
- Non-repudiation
- Long-term preservation

**Value Proposition:**
- Tamper-evidence (automatic)
- Digital signatures (non-repudiation)
- Long-term integrity (Merkle trees)

**3. Healthcare**
- Hospitals, clinics
- Health insurance companies
- Medical research organizations
- HIPAA compliance officers

**Pain Points:**
- HIPAA compliance
- Patient record integrity
- Medical research data
- Audit requirements

**Value Proposition:**
- Integrity verification (built-in)
- Audit trails (automatic)
- HIPAA compliance support

**4. Government**
- Federal agencies
- State/local governments
- Public records offices
- Transparency advocates

**Pain Points:**
- Public record integrity
- Transparency requirements
- Long-term preservation
- Audit requirements

**Value Proposition:**
- Open format (no vendor lock-in)
- Integrity verification (public trust)
- Long-term preservation

### Secondary Targets

**5. Developers & Integrators**
- Software developers
- System integrators
- Document management vendors
- Open source contributors

**Pain Points:**
- Need for document integrity
- Integration complexity
- Vendor lock-in
- Format limitations

**Value Proposition:**
- Open format (no vendor lock-in)
- Well-documented API
- Open source (MIT/Apache-2.0)
- Easy integration
